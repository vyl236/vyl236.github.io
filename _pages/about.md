Vy Luong is a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Strategic Communication department and Lab Manager of the Science Communication Identity & Impact (SCII) Research Lab. She earned her Ph.D. from the Missouri School of Journalism with a dissertation that represents the largest study to date of leadership in news organizations.

Vy’s award-winning research, teaching, and professional experience sit at the intersection of journalism and strategic communication, with expertise in leadership, identity, and organizational change. Prior to entering academia, she worked as a multimedia journalist, online editor, and media director in Oklahoma and Washington, D.C.
